Important News

Ronald Acuna Jr out for season

Arguably one of the best players in the MLB today, Ronald Acuna Jr will miss the rest of the 2024 season due to a torn ACL. The Braves won't be the same without him, but they are still a solid team without him, and they still have a good shot at making the playoffs. Acuna joins Spencer Strider as yet another Braves player who will miss the rest of the season.
